Project Narrative & Intent

For the final section of the AI Lab, I created an AI Chatbot using ChatBase. Being heavily involved in basketball coaching at the high school and college level, I recognized a massive "information overload" in the scouting process. My goal was to build a digital twin of my own thought processes and scouting reports—an AI analyst that converts raw data into actionable game plans.

Technical Implementation & Logic

I grounded the model in my proprietary methodology to ensure it followed my specific Gold Standard for player evaluation. Key technical configurations included:

  • Temperature Calibration: Dialed to the lowest setting to prioritize statistical accuracy and factual consistency over creative text generation.
  • Knowledge Base: Built on a specialized scouting dataset (CSV) containing performance metrics and qualitative notes.

The Breakthrough: Dual-Mode Architecture

The biggest challenge was getting the AI to stop acting like a trainer and start acting like a scout. Initially, it would suggest ways for opponents to improve. I resolved this through "nuclear updates" to the instruction logic, engineering a dual-mode system:

Keyword: SCOUT – Pivots the AI to aggressively hunt for weaknesses and tactical advantages.
Keyword: AUDIT – Shifts the focus to internal player development and training.
