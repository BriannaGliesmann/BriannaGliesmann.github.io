Project Narrative

As a coach at the high school and college level, I built this digital twin to cut through scouting information overload. I grounded the model in my own "Gold Standard" methodology so the AI follows my specific thought processes for player evaluation.

The Challenge & Breakthrough

The biggest challenge was shifting the AI from a "trainer" mindset to a "scout" mindset. Initially, it would suggest improvements when I needed it to hunt weaknesses. I solved this by engineering a **dual-mode system** with strict instructions:

  • Keyword: SCOUT – Pivots the AI to hunt for tactical advantages and opponent weaknesses.
  • Keyword: AUDIT – Shifts focus to internal player development and training needs.
Technical Strategy

I utilized Chatbase for the LLM integration, dialing the temperature setting way down to ensure the bot prioritized statistical accuracy over creativity. This ensures the scouting reports remain factual and precise.
