Project Overview & Goal

For the final section of the AI Lab, I created an AI Chatbot using ChatBase. I am heavily involved in basketball coaching at the high school and college level. I wanted to find a way to cut through the information overload in scouting by building a digital twin of my thought processes and reports. This is an AI analyst that turns raw data into actual game plans.

The Methodology

I started by grounding the model in my own scouting reports and methodology so it would follow my specific Gold Standard for player evaluation. I also dialed the temperature setting way down to ensure the bot stayed focused on accuracy and facts rather than getting creative with stats.

The Challenge: Scout vs. Trainer

The biggest challenge was getting the AI to stop acting like a trainer and start acting like a scout. Initially, even when I asked it to scout an opponent, it would suggest ways for them to improve. I fixed this by engineering a dual mode system with strict instructions.

Now, the logic functions through specific triggers:

  • Keyword: SCOUT – The AI pivots to hunt for weaknesses and tactical advantages.
  • Keyword: AUDIT – The AI focuses on internal player development.

It took some trial and error with the instructions and a few "nuclear updates" to the logic, but it now switches gears perfectly. This process allowed me to use my technical and conceptual skills to create a tool that makes the scouting process faster and more precise.
