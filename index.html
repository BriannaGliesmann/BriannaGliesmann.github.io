Project Narrative and Goal

I am heavily involved in basketball coaching at the high school and college level and I wanted to solve the information overload in scouting. This project is a digital twin of my scouting reports and methodology. This is an AI analyst that turns raw data into actual game plans.

The Breakthrough

The biggest challenge was shifting the AI from a trainer mindset to a scout mindset. Initially even when I asked it to scout an opponent it would suggest ways for them to improve. I solved this by engineering a dual mode system with strict instructional triggers.

It took some trial and error with the instructions and a few nuclear updates to the logic but it now switches gears perfectly. This process allowed me to use my technical and conceptual skills to create a tool that makes the scouting process faster and more precise.

Artifact 2: Machine Learning and Deep Learning Analysis

Master’s Degree Artifact 2 AI Lab

Project Narrative and Goal

In our lessons two great examples of machine learning are classification and reinforcement learning. These approaches work in very different ways depending on what we are trying to achieve and how we want the system to learn from the data. By choosing the right tool for the right job we can ensure we are working efficiently and following the path that leads to the best results for everyone. This choice is important because the way a system processes information determines how reliable and useful the outcome will be for the people who depend on it.

Classification in Medical Diagnosis

One real world application of classification which is a form of supervised learning is medical diagnosis for things like heart disease. This approach is very suitable for this problem because we can provide the system with labeled data from thousands of past patients. It can then look at specific factors like age and cholesterol levels and blood pressure to categorize a new patient as either at risk or not. This is just like using our knowledge in the lab to identify specific results based on known markers where we rely on established facts to make a clear determination.

Reinforcement learning would not be suitable for this task because it is based on trial and error. In healthcare we cannot afford to have a system experiment with a patient diagnosis to see if it eventually gets a reward for being right. We need the accuracy and safety that comes from following established categories and proven data.

Deep Learning and Autonomous Driving

For deep learning a great example is the use of deep reinforcement learning for the actual driving decisions of automated cars like merging into highway traffic. This approach is suitable because merging is a complex and changing situation that requires the car to learn through experience. The system uses deep neural networks to process all the sensor data and learns the best actions by receiving rewards for smooth merges. This allows the car to develop a kind of digital intelligence for handling unpredictable traffic and making decisions in the moment.

A simple classification approach would not be suitable here because you cannot pre label every single possible way that traffic might move. Merging requires a continuous adaptive learning process that labeling cannot handle because the environment is always shifting.
